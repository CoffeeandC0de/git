Pomodoro Method with a Twist!

The Twist: If you're in the swing of things, carry on after the timer ends! Stop only when you feel a break is needed. Take a break, then carry on with Pomodoro!

1. Decide on the task
2. Remove, pause or keep away from distractions
3. Set timer (usually 25 minutes) and begin to work!
4. Take a short break (usually 5 to 15 minutes) and restart when ready
